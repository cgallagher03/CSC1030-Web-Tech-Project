Commands

A range of instructions can be entered into the command line:

  • "QUESTION *insert name*"- Questions the chosen suspect (from Joan, John, Polly, Michael and Molly). If you are the Private Detective, you will receive more detailed information.

  • "SNIFF *insert room name*"- If you are the Police Officer, you can instruct your dog to sniff the chosen room (from bedroom, kitchen and bathroom).

  • "SWAB *insert room name*"- If you are the Forensic Scientist, you can swab the chosen room for any hidden evidence (from bedroom, kitchen and bathroom).

  • "ARREST *insert name*"- Arrest the chosen suspect to finish the game (from Joan, John, Polly, Michael and Molly). Alternatively, you can arrest using the 'Arrest Suspect' button.

  • "EXIT"- Quit game.
